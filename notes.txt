1. Intro
===================
* ES6 with Babel and Webpack has become a de-facto way to build the React applications.
* Redux created at 2015 had become a de facto Flux implementation.
* Why Redux?
  - One Store
  - Reduced Boilerplate
  - Isomorphic/Universal Friendly
  - Immutable Store
  - Hot Reloading
  - Time-travel debugging
  - Small
